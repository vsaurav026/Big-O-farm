Visitors were immersed in a shimmering world of light, shadow and color. This one was made especially for the exhibition at Rice University Art Gallery and still promised the viewer a wonderfully surreal experience. Viewers must sometimes, against their will, hear arguments, parties, and intercourse during their visit of the exhibition.
