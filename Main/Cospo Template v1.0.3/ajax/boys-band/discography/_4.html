Imagine Released: 02/5/2016

New amazing album. Our band musicians take care to bring top quality music to the fans. The greatest melodies combined with skillful beat-boxing.

This album contains several brand new arrangements of famous hits, old-school rock, swing, 90s/80s/70s hits and also some previously unreleased versions of blues compositions. We did an amazing job on the mix, making this album the most acoustic it is possible.

Each of the tracks mark a significant sound, all the pieces were recorded in high-quality studio. We made it sound as if you are sitting in the same room, sharing the experience with us. Enjoy the virtuoso guitar music of the Wix Boys in this fantastic album.
